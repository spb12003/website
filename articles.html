Why Write?

These articles and writings are primarily to help me formalize thoughts as well as to share ideas with friends and family. I find that writing is a great way to prove to myself that my ideas make sense and it helps find the holes in my thought processes and the gaps in my knowledge.

Bonds: Not a Store of Value

This article is a short research paper that uses goverments published statistics to draw the conclusion that US Issued bonds should not be considered a viable long term store of value. I believe the data and arguments in this paper also shed some light on how mismanaged and fragile our monetary system is today. The target audience is someone who has a limited background on finance and macro economics.
